Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out our contact form, including your name, business name, email address, and any message content. We do not collect payment information directly.

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide our services, send operational communications, and improve our website. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is handled in compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to decline cookies via the banner on our site. Note that declining cookies may affect certain website functionality.
