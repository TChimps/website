Pricing Questions

All plans are month-to-month by default. Enterprise clients may opt into a 6 or 12-month SLA agreement in exchange for preferential pricing and guaranteed capacity. There is no penalty for cancelling with 30 days notice on standard plans.

We will notify you proactively when you are approaching your monthly limit. Additional hours are billed at your plan overage rate (agreed upon at onboarding) or we can temporarily upgrade your plan for that month, whichever works better for your budget.
