Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Most clients are up and running within 2-3 weeks. Here's the typical timeline:
  • Week 1: Free consultation and role scoping
  • Week 2: We source and vet candidates, then assign the best match to your account
  • Week 3: Your new team member starts (we handle all HR, payroll, and equipment)
Rush timelines available for urgent needs. You can also choose to integrate our employees with your company as Outside Service Workers — they work exclusively for you while remaining on our payroll.
Our 2-Week Risk-Free Guarantee: If you're not completely satisfied within the first two weeks, we'll either provide a replacement at no cost or you can cancel with no further obligation. After the trial period, we offer free replacements within the first 90 days if performance issues arise. We're invested in finding you the right long-term fit.
All dedicated resources work during your business hours (EST or PST). This means:
  • 9am-5pm availability in your timezone
  • Real-time communication via Slack, email, or phone
  • Same-day response times for urgent requests
  • They adjust their schedule to match yours (morning meetings, afternoon check-ins, etc.)
For Enterprise Pods, we can also provide 24/7 coverage with rotating shifts.
We take security seriously:
  • NDAs & Confidentiality: All team members sign comprehensive NDAs
  • Secure Infrastructure: Company-provided laptops with VPN, encrypted storage, and monitored access
  • HIPAA/PCI Compliance: Available for healthcare and payment processing roles
  • Background Checks: Criminal and employment verification for all hires
  • Data Residency: Your data never leaves your approved systems (we don't host or store client data)
For regulated industries, we can work with your compliance team to meet specific requirements.
You have full control over day-to-day work (what they do, priorities, deadlines), while we handle all the operational overhead:

You manage: Daily tasks, project assignments, communication, and feedback

We handle: Payroll, benefits, HR issues, performance reviews, time tracking, and quality assurance

Equipment: We provide equipment by default. If you prefer, you can provide your own equipment to your team member as an option.

For Enterprise Pods, you get a dedicated team lead who manages scheduling, quality control, and escalations—like having an offshore manager on your team.
We specialize in back-office and operational roles where you need consistent, high-quality execution:
  • Billing & Finance: AR/AP coordinators, medical billing, insurance verification, collections
  • Customer Support: Technical support specialists, account managers, help desk agents
  • Admin & Operations: Executive assistants, data entry, CRM management, scheduling
  • Specialized: Bookkeepers, content writers, social media managers, QA testers
