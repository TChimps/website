Frequently Asked Questions

We take data security seriously. All client data is handled under strict confidentiality agreements. We use encrypted communications, access-controlled systems, and follow best practices aligned with Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). We never share your data with third parties.
Most clients are fully onboarded within 3–5 business days. We start with a consultation call to understand your tools, workflows, and priorities — then our team integrates with your systems and we begin delivering within the week.
Our standard coverage is Monday–Friday during North American business hours (Eastern Time). Extended and weekend coverage is available on Growth and Enterprise plans. We'll always discuss your specific needs during onboarding to ensure the right schedule.
No long-term lock-ins. We work on flexible monthly arrangements. Most clients stay because results speak for themselves — not because they're contractually obligated to. Enterprise clients may opt into SLA agreements for added guarantees.
We work with businesses across real estate, e-commerce, logistics, professional services, healthcare admin, and more. If your business has back-office, billing, or tech support needs, we can help — regardless of industry.
All plans are custom-quoted based on your volume, service areas, and hours needed. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all pricing. After your free consultation, we'll send a clear proposal within 24 hours with no hidden fees.
