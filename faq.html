Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Tech Chimps.

Tech Chimps is a Toronto-based operations outsourcing company. We handle billing, tech support, process automation, and administrative tasks for small and mid-size businesses across Canada and the United States — so founders and teams can focus on growing their business instead of running it.
We work across real estate, e-commerce, logistics, professional services, healthcare admin, and more. If your business has back-office, billing, or tech support needs, we can help — regardless of industry.
Standard coverage is Monday–Friday during North American business hours (Eastern Time). Extended evening, weekend, and overnight coverage is available on Growth and Enterprise plans as an add-on.
Yes. While we're headquartered in Toronto, we serve clients across North America. All of our services are delivered remotely, so location is no barrier. We're familiar with both Canadian and US business environments.
Packages start from $500/month for up to 20 hours of support in one service area. Our most popular Growth plan starts at $1,400/month and covers up to 60 hours across 3 service areas. Enterprise plans start at $2,800/month for full-scale coverage. All pricing is custom-scoped after a free consultation.
No. The price we quote after your consultation is exactly what you pay. We don't charge setup fees, onboarding fees, or tool licensing costs unless a specific third-party tool is required for your workflow — and we'll always discuss that upfront.
All plans are month-to-month by default. Enterprise clients may opt into a 6 or 12-month SLA agreement in exchange for preferential pricing and guaranteed capacity. There is no penalty for cancelling with 30 days' notice on standard plans.
We'll notify you proactively when you're approaching your monthly limit. Additional hours are billed at your plan's overage rate (agreed upon at onboarding), or we can temporarily upgrade your plan for that month — whichever works better for your budget.
Absolutely. You can scale up hours, add service areas, or upgrade plans at any time with 30 days' notice. Downgrading is also possible if your needs change seasonally.
Most clients are fully onboarded within 3–5 business days. We start with a free 30-minute consultation to understand your tools, workflows, and priorities — then our team integrates and begins delivering within the week.
After your free consultation, we send a short onboarding questionnaire covering your tools, workflows, and key contacts. We then hold a kickoff call to review your processes, set up access, and document your requirements. Most clients see their first deliverables within 2–3 days of the kickoff call.
We track all hours worked against your scope using time-tracking software. Starter clients receive a monthly summary; Growth and Enterprise clients receive weekly reports with a breakdown by task type. You'll always know exactly where your hours are going.
All client data is handled under strict confidentiality agreements. We use encrypted communications, access-controlled systems, and follow best practices aligned with Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). We never share your data with third parties.
Only the specific team members assigned to your account will have access to your systems — and only to the tools and data necessary to perform their tasks. Access is role-based and reviewed regularly. We can provide a full access log on request.
Yes. We work with businesses that bill across multiple legal entities, currencies, and client types. We're experienced with CAD and USD invoicing and can adapt to your existing billing platform or recommend one that fits your needs.
We build automations using Microsoft Power Automate, Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), and Google Apps Script. The right tool depends on your existing tech stack — we'll recommend the best fit during your consultation.
